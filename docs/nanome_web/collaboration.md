# Invite & Follow

### Video: Invite Users & Follow from the Web

<YoutubeEmbed videoId="6v7xlQNzzoY" title="Nanome v2.5 Quick Tips: Invite Users & Follow from the Web" />

Share your workspace with a colleague and follow their XR perspective in real time, without leaving your browser.

The tutorial covers:

- **Permissions** — Set a collaborator to viewer or editor.
- **Invite by email** — Add specific users to your workspace.
- **Follow** — Lock the web view to what the XR user sees, so you can watch them zoom into a metal-residue bond or navigate a binding site.
